# Chapter 1: Executive Summary

## Corporate Profile & Operational Background
Cosco Integrated Office Systems, Inc. is a registered stock corporation incorporated in the Philippines in December 2005. Operating from its principal office and manufacturing facility in Bacoor, Cavite, the firm engages in the manufacturing of bespoke furniture products and trading them on a wholesale and retail basis across Metro Manila and national business hubs.

## The Financial Paradox
Despite experiencing a robust 24% surge in annual sales revenue—climbing from P23.1 million to P28.7 million in 2025—the company remains in a precarious financial state. Cosco closed the 2025 fiscal year with a net operating loss of P5.58 million and carries a severe cumulative capital deficit of P29.5 million. This financial distress is driven by an inefficient cost of sales that consumes 92% of total revenues (P26.5 million), alongside heavy liabilities including P32.1 million in trade payables and an inventory bottleneck of P17.3 million tied up in raw materials. The company currently operates on financial life support, funded by P15.4 million in non-interest-bearing stockholder advances from the Bantiling family.

## Strategic Diagnosis & Choice
Through rigorous external and internal audits, Cosco's EFE Matrix (2.65) and IFE Matrix (2.55) reveal a firm characterized by high customer value and design capabilities but crippled by factory-floor inefficiencies. Quantitative Strategic Planning Matrix (QSPM) evaluations establish **Product Development (Score: 5.60)** and **Market Penetration (Score: 4.90)** as the primary strategic imperatives.

## Strategic Roadmap & Turnaround Goals
To reverse its capital deficiency and achieve sustainable profitability, Cosco must execute a turnaround roadmap centered on:
* **Revenue Expansion:** Increasing annual sales to P45 million by 2029 through aggressive bidding on major government institutional contracts, such as the P72.05 million SSS procurement plan.
* **Margin Protection & Profitability:** Transitioning from manual assembly to production automation (CNC cutting and robotics) between 2026 and 2028 to drastically reduce production costs and achieve a positive net income of P3 million by 2027.
* **Product Innovation:** Pivoting toward biophilic, ergonomic, and smart office systems under the corporate vision "Innovating the Future" to command premium pricing.
