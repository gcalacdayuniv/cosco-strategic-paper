# Chapter 2: Mission, Vision, Objectives, and Values

## Corporate Vision
**"Innovating the Future"**
To become the premier Philippine furniture manufacturer and global design leader recognized for pioneering smart, biophilic, and ergonomic workspace environments that transform professional structures.

## Corporate Mission
To design and manufacture extraordinary, inimitable, and tailored bespoke office systems that reflect our clients' unique corporate identities. We are committed to delivering sustainable value to institutional and private sector partners through expert craftsmanship, rigorous regulatory compliance, and forward-looking workplace innovation while securing financial stability and growth for our stakeholders.

## Core Values
* **Craftsmanship & Innovation:** Delivering specialized, bespoke design know-how built over 20 years of industry excellence.
* **Fiscal Integrity & Accountability:** Rebuilding operational efficiency and practicing rigorous financial discipline to overcome capital deficiencies.
* **Environmental Citizenship:** Prioritizing sustainable material sourcing, circular economy principles, and green manufacturing processes.
* **Institutional Trust & Compliance:** Maintaining uncompromising adherence to labor standards, tax regulations, and public procurement mandates.

## Strategic Objectives (2026–2029)
1. **Financial Turnaround:** Achieve a complete reversal from the 2025 net loss position (P5.58 million) to a sustainable net income of P3 million by FY 2027.
2. **Top-Line Growth:** Increase baseline annual sales revenue from P28.7 million to P45 million by FY 2029.
3. **Cost Structure Optimization:** Lower the cost-of-sales ratio from 92% of revenue down to target industry norms via production automation by 2028.
4. **Deleveraging & Liquidity:** Reduce trade payables (P32.1 million) by 25% over three consecutive years through improved inventory turnover of the P17.3 million raw material stockpile.
