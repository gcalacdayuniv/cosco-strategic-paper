## Functional Audit
* **Management:** Strong leadership under President Jose P. Bantiling, Treasurer Elenita P. Bantiling, and COO Linda V. Dela Cruz overseeing planning, staffing, and control across a lean workforce of 26 employees.
* **Marketing & Sales:** Achieved P28.7 million in sales in 2025 (+24%) through market penetration and targeting institutional clients like the SSS, but pricing models currently fail to protect gross margins.
* **Accounting & Finance:** Severe financial distress marked by a P29.5 million cumulative deficit, P5.58 million net loss in 2025, and P32.1 million in trade payables. Operations depend heavily on P15.4 million in non-interest-bearing stockholder advances. Cost of sales consumes 92% of revenue.
* **Production & Operations:** Operates an integrated facility in Bacoor. Direct labor dropped from P6.9M to P2.6M in 2025, yet material costs and an inventory bottleneck (P17.3M) tie up vital working capital.
* **Research & Development / MIS:** Guided by "Innovating the Future," R&D focuses on biophilic and ergonomic setups. However, the firm lacks an integrated ERP/SAP system to optimize inventory turnover against sales forecasts.
