# Chapter 8: Strategy Review, Evaluation, and Control

## Strategy Evaluation Framework
Cosco evaluates operational execution against Richard Rumelt’s Four Criteria for Strategy Evaluation:
1. **Consistency:** Ensuring sales bidding policies do not conflict with corporate gross margin requirements.
2. **Consonance:** Verifying that product development continuously adapts to external workplace ergonomic and biophilic trends.
3. **Feasibility:** Auditing financial solvency to confirm capital investments do not overextend stockholder advances.
4. **Advantage:** Protecting core bespoke design capabilities against competitor imitation.

## Reviewing Bases of Strategy
* **Quarterly IFE/EFE Audits:** Re-evaluating external inflation rates, import tariffs, and internal cost-of-sales ratios every 90 days.
* **Competitor Benchmarking:** Monitoring strategic moves of Volume Wholesalers and Global Design Leaders during national trade expos.

## Measuring Company Performance: The Balanced Scorecard
To move beyond purely retrospective accounting numbers, Cosco tracks performance across four strategic perspectives:

| Perspective | Strategic KPI | Target | Frequency |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Financial** | Cost-of-Sales Ratio<br>Net Profit Margin | $< 75\%$ of Revenue<br>$\ge 8\%$ Net Income | Monthly |
| **Customer** | Institutional Bid Win Rate<br>Client Retention Rate | $\ge 35\%$ on Gov't Bids<br>$\ge 85\%$ Repeat Corporate Clients | Quarterly |
| **Internal Operations** | Raw Material Inventory Turnover<br>Factory Scrap & Waste Rate | $\ge 4.0\times$ annually<br>$< 5\%$ material waste | Monthly |
| **Learning & Growth** | CNC Automation Adoption Rate<br>Employee Upskilling Hours | $100\%$ production line integration<br>40 hours/employee annually | Semi-Annually |

## Corrective Actions Matrix
If performance deviates from budgeted targets, management will trigger immediate corrective controls:
* **Trigger:** Cost of sales remains above 85% for two consecutive quarters.<br>**Action:** Institute an immediate freeze on administrative hiring and renegotiate supplier credit terms.
* **Trigger:** Inventory turnover falls below 2.0x.<br>**Action:** Launch discounted promotional bundles through digital trading channels to convert idle stock into working capital.

## Contingency Planning
1. **Severe Supply Chain Disruption / Import Tariffs:** If U.S. tariff shifts or hardwood shortages occur, Cosco will immediately shift 50% of production volume to locally sourced engineered bamboo and recycled steel composites.
2. **Major Institutional Bid Loss:** If primary government contracts (e.g., SSS renewals) are lost to volume competitors, Cosco will reallocate manufacturing capacity to small-to-medium enterprise (SME) compact hybrid workspace packages.
3. **Withdrawal of Stockholder Advances:** If related-party capital support is restricted, Cosco will divest non-core trading assets and warehouse equipment to satisfy trade payables.

## Comprehensive Strategic Audit
An annual independent strategic audit will verify that financial reporting (AFS), legal compliance (SEC GIS), and environmental waste management strictly adhere to state regulations while driving the firm toward its 2029 vision of "Innovating the Future."
